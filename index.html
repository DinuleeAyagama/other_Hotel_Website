Fine Dining Experience

Savor a variety of gourmet dishes prepared by our expert chefs using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Choose from an exquisite selection of international and traditional Sri Lankan cuisine.

Surrounded by tropical landscapes, our jungle-fringed swimming pool provides a refreshing escape. Guests can explore scenic hiking trails, cycle through charming villages, or take part in wildlife spotting and birdwatching adventures.
